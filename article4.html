HEAR YOURSELF THINK

Take this seriously please.

Till you hear your own voice in your thoughts or thinking you're not at the level of cogitation that you need to be to move to the next phase of your transformation and emergence.

For as long as the voice you hear on the inside is not recognisable to you are feeding on suggested or projected thoughts. These often do not get you to the point of change though they may have some impact on the process.

This is why you must learn to find time to put off the television set and other screens, including phones and such devices. You must introspect in the quietness of your exclusive space. You must hear yourself think.

I discovered that our minds react with the most dynamism at this depth of thinking. Now you know why religious and mystical societies teach meditation as some sort of rule. Shut the world out and hear your voice as your mind engages the information stored, or that is being inspired in you.

Your next level depends on this. As a man thinks...so is he.
